Stay Cautious!
Should I buy things in person or should order online?
    Governors are responsible for making decisions regarding statewide closures, but to practice proper social distancing, it is best to order online to help prevent contamination or spread of the Coronavirus.

Should I stockpile on supplies?
    Our opinion is that you should NOT stockpile on supplies because it is predicted that stores will continually receive stock of items. Therefore, for the good of society, please refrain from stockpiling - buy what you need, not too much more.
What should I do with incoming mail or packages?
    After picking up mail, make sure to either let it sit untouched in a secluded and designated area for three days or scan all faces of each item under UV-C Light to kill any viruses. If items must be moved, please wash your hands afterwards and make sure to clean any applicable surfaces with 70% Isopropyl Alcohol for sanitation.
