Hygiene

Stay Clean!
What can I do to maintain proper hygiene at home?
    Trim your nails, shower daily, wash your hair, floss, brush your teeth, comb your hair, trim your fingernails and toenails, and wear fresh clothes daily.
What can I do to maintain hygiene regarding hair?
    Wash your hair, comb it, and keep it clean. You can also give yourself a simple hair “trim” to keep your hair from going awry.

What kinds of hygiene practices should I follow regarding going outside?
    Specifically due to Covid-19, wash your hands, wear a mask (if applicable), don’t come into contact with others, and don’t touch others.

You should be following hygiene practices akin to those before Covid-19- if anything, you should be more detail oriented and careful.

Note: Maintain hygiene even if you are not leaving your residence.
